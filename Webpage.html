> MADURAI_

Welcome to Madurai — one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in India, located on the banks of the Vaigai River in Tamil Nadu.

> ABOUT THE CITY

Madurai is often called the "Athens of the East" and is famous as a major center of Tamil culture, learning, and temple architecture. The city has a history spanning over 2,500 years and was once the capital of the Pandya Kingdom.

> MEENAKSHI AMMAN TEMPLE

The heart of Madurai is the Meenakshi Amman Temple, dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi and Lord Sundareswarar. Known for its towering gopurams covered in thousands of colorful sculptures, it attracts millions of devotees and tourists every year.

> CULTURE & FOOD

  • Famous for Jigarthanda, a traditional cool drink
  • Home to the historic Thirumalai Nayakkar Mahal
  • Known for jasmine flowers (Madurai Malli)
  • Rich tradition of Tamil literature and Sangam-era history
